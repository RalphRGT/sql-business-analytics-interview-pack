# SQL Business Analytics Interview Pack

## Overview
This project is a SQL analytics practice pack built using a small sales database.  
The aim is to demonstrate practical SQL skills used in Data Analyst, BI Analyst, Business Analyst and Reporting Analyst roles.

The project covers business KPI analysis, customer analysis, regional performance, product profitability, sales channel performance, and interview-style SQL queries using CTEs and window functions.

## Database Structure

The database contains five tables:

- Customers
- Products
- Orders
- OrderDetails
- SalesReps

## SQL Skills Demonstrated

- SELECT statements
- JOINs
- GROUP BY aggregations
- Subqueries
- Common Table Expressions
- Window functions
- RANK()
- ROW_NUMBER()
- LAG()
- Moving average analysis
- Business KPI calculations

## Business Questions Answered

1. What are the total sales, cost, profit and gross margin?
2. Which regions generate the highest revenue?
3. Which product categories perform best?
4. What is the monthly sales trend?
5. What are the top 5 products by sales?
6. What is the average order value?
7. Who are the highest-value customers?
8. Which sales channels perform best?
9. How are sales reps performing by region?
10. Which products generate the most profit?
11. How do products rank by sales?
12. What are the top 3 products per region?
13. What is the month-on-month sales growth?
14. What is the 3-month moving average sales trend?
15. Which customers are repeat customers?
